Conclusion

In this Webquest, you have explored and defined creativity. You have also had the opportunity to develop a new skill of your choice. Using this newfound knowledge, you have created a new challenge to help someone else develop his or her skills as well. In doing so, you analyzed its value, evaluated the ideas of your peers and created a new product. This is known as the creative process.

Since you have completed the project, here are some reflection questions to take your learning further.

  • Has your perception of creativity changed? If so, how?
  • Where do you see creativity in your daily life?
  • How does it relate to our world?
