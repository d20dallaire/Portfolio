Evaluation

The following rubric reflects the student’s work through the whole creative process of this WebQuest project. The rubric below is separated into four levels of achievement instead of a point or percentage system. To determine the student’s final grade, the teacher averages out the different levels of achievement attained. For example, if on the rubric the student falls on a 3, 4, 3, 3, 4, 3, the student’s final grade would be a 3 because there are more 3s obtained. In the end, the teacher uses their professional judgement to justify the grade.

Percentages were added to assist teachers in understanding the meaning of each level of achievement.

Typically, with appropriate support, all students should be able to achieve grade-level expectations.
