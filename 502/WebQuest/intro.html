Introduction

What do you think of when you hear the word creativity. You have probably already heard of the word creativity but what does it mean and how does it connect to my learning?

Here are a few case scenarios were you will need to use your creativity:

  • You are presented with a problem and need to come up with solutions.
  • You company needs help to improve their advertising campaign.
  • You are making a recipe and are missing a few ingredients.
  • Designing a title page, a logo, packaging for a product, a game, etc.
  • Generating themes for a charity or a fundraising campaign.
  • Come up with original ideas or to promote new inventions.
  • Make your dreams into reality.
  • Need to plan a celebration, assembly or social event.
  • Meet challenges at a Science Fair.
  • Brainstorming ways to cut energy use.
  • Generating interview questions.
  • Redefining job responsibilities and roles in the classroom or at work.

As you go through this WebQuest journey, you will be exploring more deeply into the matter of creativity and discover your own creative process.
