The Central Question

 

The central question that will be explored through this WebQuest activity as follows:

What does it mean to be creative and how does this apply to my learning?

Student Instructions:

This activity is organized into 5 sections. 

  • Introduction: Outlines the WebQuest and central question
  • Task: Details the work to be completed
  • Process: Explains the steps to take to complete the task
  • Evaluation: Rubric detailing how you will be marked
  • Conclusion: Summary of our learning and future reflections

Teacher Instructions:

If you are an educator, a lesson plan detailing this activity is provided and can be accessed, reused or modified.
