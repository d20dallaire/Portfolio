Task

Did you know that you were born with natural creative potentiel? During your first few years of life, your imagination is at full force as you play, imitate and understand the world around you. As you grow up, it is possible to lose that creative flow. Creativity is a skill that is never lost; it is a skill that everyone possesses. The strength of this skill is dependent on how often you use it in your daily life. Building upon this ability will allow you to do so much more in your life as it offers limitless possibilities in your personal or professional life. This WebQuest will enable you to experience a creative process for which the outcome is unknown.

This process you will experience is separated into 6 tasks:

  1. Background research on creativity.
  2. Define and share online what creativity means to you.
  3. Gain a new skill badge online through Kids Learning Skills and Being Awesome website.
  4. Analyze and explain how learning a new skills connects to creativity.
  5. Propose and share a new project based on your new skill. Peer review your project and two other proposed projects using an evaluation rubric.
  6. Submit your completed creative project for grading.

Further details on each area is located in the process section of this WebQuest.
