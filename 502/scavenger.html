"Cereal" Plagiarism Scavenger Hunt

Mission Details

Are you ready for a new adventure?

The SECRET RECIPE for a brand new cereal has been hacked from the Wheat Factory Systems Inc.

You must retrace the digital footprints left by the cereal thieves in order to collect and retrieve parts of the FINAL PASSWORD.

Along the way, you will encounter security questions. You must answer correctly before being able to move on to the next security question.

Ironically, the security questions you are going to encounter are all based on PLAGIARISM.

Please read carefully the following instructions.
