Learner Description:

This virtual field trip is designed for High School students taking a computer science option course at the Calgary French and International School. This option course has been developped for students with a curiosity for game design and game development.


About Your Tour

This site provides you with the opportunity to explore and discover some of the larger Canadian gaming companies. You are free to explore each company using the videos and links provided. At the end of this activity, you will need to have completed two assignments. The first assigment (Part A) is a fill in the blanks chart of basic information regarding each company. The second assignment (Part B) is a reflective video response through Flipgrid. Both assignments, and the answer key for part A, are located in the "Assignments" tab above.

Instructions:


  1. Check out the assignments page before exploring the websites.
  2. Open and make a copy of the Part A: Gaming Company Summary.
  3. Fill out and complete the summary as you explore the content.
  4. Review summary answer key.
  5. Create a video to answer the reflection question and post it using Flipgrid.
