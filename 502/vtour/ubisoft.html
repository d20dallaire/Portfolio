About Ubisoft

Ubisoft is a successful international french video game publisher. As the fourth largest publicly-traded game company in the Americas and Europe you have probably already heard of or experienced many of its games for yourself. Ubisoft Montréal, Québec was founded in 1997 and now employs more than 3000 employees.
