Welcome to my EDTECH e-Portfolio webpage. This website space allows access to the accumulated artifacts I have created throughout my online course journey experience in the Master's of Educational Technology program with a certificate in Games and Simulations at the Boise State University in Idaho.

About Me

I have been teaching for the past 9 years in Alberta, Canada. All of which I have experience teaching a variety of subject matters in French both at the elementary, and secondary level.

Last year, I was given the fortuitous opportunity to work in the private sector at the Calgary French and International School where I met the school's Director of Innovation and Technology. She became aware of my desire to find a master's program that was more in line with my interests. Knowing my goals and passion for innovative teaching, she inform me of the MET program and its benefits as she saw them, having been a recent graduate herself.

As a self-proclaimed geek, games, and technology of all sorts have always been a part of my life. 

These collective experiences have sparked a desire in me to seek progressive and creative teaching methods in the fields of education and technology.
